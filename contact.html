About "Walk My Dog"

"Walk My Dog" is a service to do just that.....walk your dog and offer pet sitting services while you are away. You may not have time because of employment, surgery, out of town trips, or just simply, there aren't enough hours in the day to walk your pet. That's why I'm here. Other services will be available as well to care for your pets, including cats. Exotic pets are negotiable. One certainty I can give you: I will give your pet the best care.

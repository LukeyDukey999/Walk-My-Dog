Walk My Dog Rates

Single Visit: $15.00. This rate includes servicing one dog, no less than a half an hour, no more than an hour. This designated time is dedicated to the needs of your dog, i.e. walking, letting outside in fenced area, water/feeding, medications given(if applies)- basically, making sure your pet is a happy one when you come home.

Additional Dogs

If the dog is in the same house hold it is an additional $5.00. The same needs stated above will be met for the all dogs.

Payment is expected on the day of service, unless negotiated between myself and the client.
